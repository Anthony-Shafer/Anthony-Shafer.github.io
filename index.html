About Me

Hello, my name is Byansi Anthony and I am a passionate UX designer with 1 year of experience in creating user-centered digital experiences. My expertise lies in conducting user research, creating wireframes and prototypes, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to develop intuitive and visually appealing designs.

My goal has always been to create products and services that not only meet user needs, but also exceed their expectations. I have a strong understanding of the principles of design and usability, and I use this knowledge to create experiences that are both functional and delightful.

I am excited to continue using my skills and expertise to make a difference in the field of UX design. Thank you for considering my qualifications and experience

Skills

Skill Description
User research I have experience conducting various user research methods, including surveys, interviews, and usability testing. I am skilled in analyzing research data and using insights to inform design decisions.
Wireframing and prototyping I am proficient in creating wireframes and prototypes using various tools, including Sketch, Figma, and Adobe XD. I can quickly iterate on designs and create interactive prototypes to test with users.
Interaction design I have a strong understanding of interaction design principles and can create engaging and intuitive interactions for digital products and services. I am skilled in creating user flows, animations, and microinteractions that enhance the user experience.
Visual design I have a good eye for design and can create visually appealing designs that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. I am skilled in using typography, color, and layout to create designs that align with brand guidelines and user needs.
Information architecture I have experience creating site maps, navigation systems, and other information design artifacts to help users find and use content. I can organize and structure content in a way that makes sense to users and enhances the overall user experience.
Usability testing I am skilled in planning and conducting usability testing to evaluate the effectiveness of designs. I can create test plans, recruit participants, and facilitate testing sessions to gather feedback and identify areas for improvement.
Collaborative mindset I am a strong collaborator and enjoy working with cross-functional teams to create user-centered designs. I am skilled in communicating design decisions and rationale to stakeholders and can work effectively with developers and other team members to bring designs to life.
